ID System

Each note has a unique ID formed by a UTC timestamp of its date of creation combined with a three-letter random sequence. Notes link to related notes. There is no other structure besides that. Better to dive in and see where the rabbithole takes you.

Archive Policy/License

Ideas persist by repetition more than preservation. Still, feel free to archive this page in places like Wayback Machine. You can also download, share, repost, and rehost the contents, so long as you give me attribution for any original portions.

FAQ

  1. Hey, isn't this similar to that other guy?
    Imitation is the sincerest form of flattery that mediocrity can pay to greatness.
  2. Why?
    I'm lucky to have "insatiable curiosity" as a character trait. I'm also lucky to be alive at a time when information is readily accessible. We live in an infinite universe of fractal complexity. Much like George Mallory and Everest, it begs to be explored. Putting it all here gives me organization and a "garden of knowledge" I can tend to in hopes it will bloom.
  3. Why the pseudonym?
    Cause people on the internet are fucking weirdos. I'd also like to write things I don't want tied to my real name via Google Search. I'm not against breaking the veil for the right people. I strongly believe in the search query theory of online writing, so don't be afraid to reach out if something I've written resonates with you.
  4. I found something you're wrong about.
    Cool! Send me an email or a DM with an explanation and I'll happily credit you however you like.
  5. How dare you! You've written something I find problematic.
    I'm sorry you feel that way. Refer to the previous bullet or I don't have time for you.
  6. I like your stuff! Where else can I find you?
    Follow me on Twitter! I try to keep my feed high-signal, so it's mainly reading notes, shared links, and threads-of-threads on topics I cover here.
