Morning Read: The Relativity of Wrong

Today's morning read is an essay titled The Relativity of Wrong by Isaac Asimov, esteemed scientist, professor, and science fiction author. Even if you've never read the essay, you've likely heard the following quote:

My answer to him was, "John, when people thought the Earth was flat, they were wrong. When people thought the Earth was spherical, they were wrong. But if you think that thinking the Earth is spherical is just as wrong as thinking the Earth is flat, then your view is wronger than both of them put together."

Worth the read and an important reminder of how we think about truth & knowledge in science.
